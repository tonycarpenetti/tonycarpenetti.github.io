    A way to remember what you almost forgot.

    Memory Jogger follows the "Abecedarius " mneumonic acronym technique to help you remember something.
    It goes through each letter of the alphabet one letter at a time giving example words to jog your memory.

    If you know what letter the thing you're trying to remember starts with, you can select it below.
    Otherwise, go down to "START."
